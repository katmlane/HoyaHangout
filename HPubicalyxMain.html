Plant Characteristic Description
Species H. pubicalyx
Name Origin Pubicalyx comes from 'pubic' meaning downy and 'calyx' being the outermost part of the flower.
Origin The Philippines
Leaf Morphology Deep green lanceolate leaves with silver or grey flecks on the top side.
Flower Morphology Number of different cultivars of H. pubicalyx with different colored flowers. The flowers are revolute purple or dark pink with a lighter pink star-shaped center with yellow accents.
Flowering Schedule Flowers typically produced in spring and summer.
Maximum Size Over 3-6 feet indoors.
Humidity Loves high humidity but can tolerate lower humidity levels.
Temperature Thrives best at 50 - 77 degrees Fahrenheit.
