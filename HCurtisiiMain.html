Species H. Curtisii
Name Origin Curtisii is named after botanist Charles Curtis.
Origin Southeast Asia across Thailand, the Philippines, Borneo, and Malaysia
Leaf Morphology Elliptic, dark green leaves with silver or grey speckling.
Flower Morphology Reflexed cream or yellow flower with red centers and yellow accents.
Flowering Schedule Flowers typically produced in spring and summer.
Maximum Size Over 3-6 feet indoors.
Humidity Loves high humidity but can tolerate lower humidity levels.
Temperature Thrives best 60 - 95 degrees Fahrenheit.
